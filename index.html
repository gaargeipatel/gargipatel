About Me

Hello! I'm Gargi, a product designer based in New Delhi, India. My journey into design began with a simple curiosity: why do some things feel so right to use, while others are a constant struggle?

I believe that great design is a blend of empathy, problem-solving, and a keen eye for aesthetics. I'm always eager to learn and grow, whether it's through a new project, a workshop, or just sketching out an idea on my tablet. When I'm not designing, you'll find me exploring new places or experimenting with new recipes.
