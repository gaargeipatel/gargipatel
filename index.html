About Me

Hello, I’m Gargi a UX designer with a foundation in engineering, data science, and user-centered design. My approach to problem-solving blends analytical thinking with empathy.

Coming from a data science background, I’m always curious about the story behind the numbers where they come from, why patterns exist, and what they reveal about real human experiences. As a UX designer, I go beyond what users say on the surface to understand the emotions, motivations, and unseen challenges beneath.

This mix of data understanding and human insight helps me design solutions that are not only usable, but genuinely meaningful and impactful. I enjoy simplifying complex problems, bridging design with technology, and creating experiences that make life a little easier for people.
