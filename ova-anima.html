Proposed Concepts

Designed for all women, offering a comprehensive solution to manage and maintain a healthy lifestyle. Whether it's women with PCOS or those seeking to track their period cycles and adopt healthier habits, the hybrid solution—a mobile app paired with an optional wearable device—caters to diverse needs.

The app provides   personalized tracking for symptoms, period cycles, lifestyle metrics (exercise, sleep, and nutrition), and incorporates gamification to encourage consistency. A community-driven approach fosters connection through user maps, direct messaging, and shared goals, promoting motivation and emotional well-being.

AIM of Project

This project aims to support all women , whether they have PCOS or not, by spreading awareness about its root causes. By educating women on lifestyle management, the goal is to empower them to take proactive steps in maintaining their health and preventing PCOS related complications.
